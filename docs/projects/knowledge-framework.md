In the age of AI and beyond, knowledge will not just be about possession but about accessibility, adaptability, and contextual application. - **Passive consumption**: Learning disconnected from application and creation
- **Individual silos**: Competition rather than collaborative knowledge building
- **Gatekeeping**: Access barriers to essential knowledge
